Sorter for Ace Attorney characters. Pick your sources, hit the Start button and let the trial begin!

There are nearly 300 characters in Ace Attorney, so the sorting may take quite a while. Be sure to brew a cup of dark and bitter coffee or pour your finest vintage into your finest chalice!

Click on the character you like better from the two, or tie them if you like or dislike them equally



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the first/second/third buttons.



Inspired by and based on Touhou Character Sorter.
